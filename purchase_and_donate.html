Let's Address the Elephant in the Room

Asian elephants are huge draw for tourists visiting Asia each year. Some experiences allow people to see animals in the wild, while others take place in more controlled settings with captive elephants. Done responsibly, tourism brings tremendous benefits for people and wildlife. Done poorly, it can threaten the very species we love.

Conservation means taking care of people too

An unprecedented economic crisis in Sri Lanka has led to fuel shortages and skyrocketing inflation. Rural households are struggling. We're providing relief supplies to families that are living below the poverty line.
